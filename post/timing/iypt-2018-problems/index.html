Problem No.1 Invent Yourself

Construct a simple seismograph that amplifies a local disturbance by mechanical, optical or electrical methods.
Determine the typical response curve of your device and investigate the parameters of the damping constant.
What is the maximum amplification that you can achieve?
通过力学、光学、电学方法放大局部扰动以构建一个简单的地震仪.
测定设备的响应曲线并分析影响其阻尼常数的参量.
你能达到的最大放大倍率是多少?

Problem No.2 Colour of Powders 粉末的颜色

If a coloured material is ground to a powder, in some cases the resulting powder may have a different colour to that of the original material.
Investigate how the degree of grinding affects the apparent colour of the powder.
若将有色材料研磨成粉末状,在某些情况下,所得粉末的颜色与原材料颜色不同.
研究研磨程度对于粉末颜色的影响.

Problem No.3 Dancing Coin 跳舞的硬币

Take a strongly cooled bottle and put a coin on its neck.
Over time you will hear a noise and see movements of the coin.
Explain this phenomenon and investigate how the relevant parameters affect the dance.
在一个强冷却的瓶子颈部放置一枚硬币.
一段时间后你将听到一个声音并观察到硬币运动.
解释该现象并研究相关参量对其的影响.

Problem No.4 Heron’s Fountain Heron喷泉

Construct a Heron’s fountain and explain how it works.
Investigate how the relevant parameters affect the height of the water jet.
制作一个Heron喷泉并解释其工作原理.
分析各参量对于喷泉高度的影响.

Wikipedia: Heron’s Fountian

Problem No.5 Drinking Straw 吸管

When a drinking straw is placed in a glass of carbonated drink, it can rise up, sometimes toppling over the edge of the glass.
Investigate and explain the motion of the straw and determine the conditions under which the straw will topple.
当一根吸管放置于碳酸饮料中时,它可能会上升,有时会翻倒在杯壁上.
研究并解释吸管的运动,并确定吸管翻倒的条件.

Problem No.6 Ring Oiler 抛油环

An oiled horizontal cylindrical shaft rotates around its axis at constant speed.
Make a ring from a cardboard disc with the inner diameter roughly twice the diameter of the shaft and put the ring on the shaft.
Depending on the tilt of the ring, it can travel along the shaft in either direction.
Investigate the phenomenon.
一个油的水平的圆柱体轴以一定的速度绕着它的轴旋转.
用硬纸板做一个内径大约是柱体直径两倍的环,并把环放在柱体上.
根据环的倾斜程度,它可以沿柱体以任意方向旋转.
探究这一现象.

Wikipedia: Ring oiler

Problem No.7 Conical Piles 锥形堆

Non-adhesive granular materials can be poured such that they form a cone-like pile.
Investigate the parameters that affect the formation of the cone and the angle it makes with the ground.
无粘性颗粒材料在倾倒时可以形成一种锥形堆.
研究各参数对于椎体形成及其与地面所成角的影响.

Problem No.8 Cusps in a Cylinder

A horizontal cylinder is partially filled with a viscous fluid.
When the cylinder is rotated around its axis, unusual fluid behaviour can be observed, such as cusp-like shapes on the walls of the cylinder.
Investigate the phenomenon.
有一部分填充有黏滞液体的水平圆筒.
其在绕其轴线旋转时,可以观察到一种异样的流动,例如在柱体壁上的尖锥状.
研究该现象.

Problem No.9 Candle in Water 水中烛

Add some weight to a candle such that it barely floats in water.
As the candle burns, it may continue to float.
Investigate and explain this phenomenon.
给蜡烛附加一些重量,使其几乎无法在水中漂浮.
在蜡烛燃烧时,它有可能重新漂浮起来.
研究并解释该现象.

Problem No.10 Tesla Valve 特斯拉阀门

A Tesla valve is a fixed-geometry, passive, one-direction valve.
A Tesla valve offers a resistance to flow that is much greater in one direction compared to the other.
Create such a Tesla valve and investigate its relevant parameters.
特斯拉阀门是一种固定几何形状、无源的单向阀门.
它提供了一种单向较强的流动阻力.
制作一个特斯拉阀门并研究相关参量.

Wikipedia: Tesla valve

Problem No.11 Azimuthal-Radial Pendulum 方位角径向摆

Fix one end of a horizontal elastic rod to a rigid stand.
Support the other end of the rod with a taut string to avoid vertical deflection and suspend a bob from it on another string (see figure).
In the resulting pendulum the radial oscillations (parallel to the rod) can spontaneously convert into azimuthal oscillations (perpendicular to the rod) and vice versa.
Investigate the phenomenon.
将水平弹性杆的一段固定到刚性支架上.
另一端使用绷紧的绳固定以避免垂直偏转,并在在该侧使用另一根绳挂一个摆球(如图).
摆锤的径向震荡(平行于杆)可以自发地转换成方位震荡(垂直于杆),反之亦然.
研究该现象.

Problem No.12 Curie Point Engine 居里点引擎

Make a nickel disc that can rotate freely around its axis.
Place a magnet near the edge of the disc and heat this side of it.
The disc starts to rotate.
Investigate the parameters affecting the rotation and optimize the design for a steady motion.
制作一个可以绕其轴线自由旋转的镍盘.
在镍盘的边缘放置一个磁铁,并加热这一端.
镍盘会开始旋转.
研究相关参量对于该转动的影响,并优化以取得稳定运动.

Wikipedia: Curie temperature

Problem No.13 Weighing Time 称量时间

It is commonly known that an hourglass changes its weight (as measured by a scale) while flowing.
Investigate this phenomenon.
众所周知,时沙漏在流动时其质量会发生该改变(用天平测量).
研究该现象.

Problem No.14 Radiant Lantern 辐射之灯

When taking a picture of a glowing lantern at night, a number of rays emanating from the centre of the lantern may appear in the pictures.
Explain and investigate this phenomenon.
在夜晚拍摄发光的灯笼的照片时,可能会拍摄到从灯笼中心发射出的数条射线.
解释并研究该现象.

Problem No.15 Blowing Bubbles 吹泡泡

When blowing on a soap film in a ring, a bubble may be formed.
The liquid film may pop or continue to exist.
Investigate how the number of bubbles produced from a single soap film and the characteristics of the bubbles depend on the relevant parameters.
对位于圆环中的皂膜吹气,可能会形成气泡.
液膜有可能破裂或继续存在.
研究相关参数对于单一皂膜产生气泡数量和气泡性质的影响.

Problem No.16 Acoustic Levitation 声悬浮

Small objects can levitate in acoustic standing waves.
Investigate the phenomenon.
To what extent can you manipulate the objects?
小的物体可以在驻波中悬浮.
研究该现象.
你可以在多大程度上与物体交互?

Problem No.17 Water Bottle 水瓶

The current craze of water bottle flipping involves launching a partially filled plastic bottle into the air so that it performs a somersault before landing on a horizontal surface in a stable, upright position.
Investigate the phenomenon and determine the parameters that will result in a successful flip.
目前火热的翻转水瓶,即将一个部分填充的塑料瓶丢向空中,使其空中翻转,最后平稳地立在水平面上.
研究该现象并得出成功翻转的参数.
